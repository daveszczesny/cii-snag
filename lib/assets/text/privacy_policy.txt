Construction It Is (“CII,” “we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application (Construct RenoMate).

​3. Data Sharing

5. User Rights
Since we do not collect personal data, you do not need to request access or deletion from us. Any data in the app remains on your device.

No data is transmitted, stored externally, or shared with us or any third party. All analytics remain fully local and anonymous.
